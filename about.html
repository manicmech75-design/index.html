About Flip City

Flip City is an independent sports card content site focused on card values, trends, market movement, and collector education.

We publish information about sports trading cards including rookies, modern inserts, graded cards, and long-term collectible trends.

Flip City is not affiliated with eBay, PSA, Fanatics, Topps, Panini, or any card manufacturer.

Content on this site is for informational and entertainment purposes only.
